Yes. Files are built for Creative Cloud. You’ll need an active Adobe plan for the apps you use.

How does the 14-day refund work?

If your first payment isn’t a fit, email support within 14 days for a full refund. After that, standard billing applies—see Terms for edge cases.

Is my payment information secure?

We never store your full card on our servers. Checkout runs through PCI-compliant payment providers over encrypted TLS.

Can I use assets for commercial client work?

Yes—your membership covers commercial use for work you create. You can’t resell or redistribute the raw source files.

What if I cancel?

New packs publish on the same day each month. You’ll get an email and they appear at the top of your dashboard.
